#BiharElection #Niteshyadav

The Bihar elections witnessed significant political activity, with major parties vying for dominance in a highly competitive environment. A key highlight was Operation Sindoor, a strategic campaign by the BJP to strengthen its influence by targeting women voters and socially influential groups through welfare narratives and symbolism. This move was seen as an attempt to counter regional parties' hold on caste-based politics. The election reflected evolving voter priorities, focusing on development and governance alongside traditional identity politics.
